Assets

  1. Video should be delivered as mp4 at a size of 280 (w) x 300 (h).
  2. Performace is critical. Each video should be no longer than 10-15 seconds in length (the shorter the better).
  3. Video should strive to meet a file size greater than 1.5MB each. Remember, each video will add to the cumulitive weight of the page, so size must be kept low.

Note

  1. Video utilizes prefers-reduced-motion in addition to the pause button. When user disables animation in their operating system, these videos will not autoplay.
  2. Video may not play if mobile device is in low-power mode. This is for a good reason—so video does not drain the battery life.
