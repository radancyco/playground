Personal Banking & Wealth Management (PBWM) Technology

Citi’s PBWM organization has a streamlined, strategic focus on U.S. Personal Banking – inclusive of Branded Cards, Retail Services, and U.S. Retail Banking. PBWM also includes the Global Wealth Management sector, which encompasses our consumer wealth businesses in North America, Asia, Latin America, EMEA, and the Global Citi Private Bank.

Our Technology teams within PBWM diligently work behind the scenes to deliver best-in-class service for our customers. And as client expectations continue to evolve rapidly, our unique model is well-positioned to evolve with them. We deliver software that has a variety of end users: mobile apps and websites that help our customers manage their money; desktop software that allows our customer service agents to provide support and advice; and internal databases, microservices, and tools that Citi teams rely upon every day. To support our innovative work, PBWM Technology has a variety of roles, from software development and data analytics to product management, business analysis, process re-engineering, and more.

No matter what team you join within PBWM Technology, you’ll be solving complex challenges in a culture committed to equality and equity alongside great people, including the remarkable women in technology at Citi who deliver game-changing solutions every day. Whatever your role, and no matter your personal identity, you will have a hand in leading the charge on innovation with us.

Over 28 million mobile users on applications supported by the PBWM Technology team at Citi

To help enable growth and progress for Citi’s clients around the world, our team is accelerating the future of banking through industry-leading technology. To do this, we put the client at the core of what we do – every decision we make, every experience we create, and every interaction we have. We also embrace agility and modern engineering to deliver well-designed services that are simple, always available, and integrated into our clients’ increasingly digital lifestyles.

Our Work

Enhanced Customer Engagement Program

Our Citi Self Invest platform enables growth and progress by allowing clients to meet and achieve their long-term financial goals through a seamless end-to-end digital experience. Our PBWM Technology team has partnered with our Risk and Control partners to simplify Account Opening processes and modernize the architecture to uplift the technologies. The teams built the foundation to support future roadmap and infrastructure needs to scale the application to achieve future demands. The results of this Citi-built browser trading experience include enhanced engagement through a unified browsing and search experience for Trading, Insights and News, and provides clients with a deeper understanding of how they can build wealth with us.

Decision Engine Optimization

Our Personal Installment Lending project is a prime example of tech stack modernization and simplification that will see Citi move to a robust and strategic infrastructure. The purpose of the project is to move to a new Credit Decision engine and core processing platform for the installment lending product, which will enable Citi to give out personal installment loans to mass market customers for whatever their financial needs. As well as being a major win for customers, the project is elevating the profile of our teams with senior management, exposing employees to new learning experiences, and bringing teams together to build new relationships and celebrate their hard work and success.

UI Redesign

The core vision of this project was to revamp the banking experience for our customers in order to make their lives easier by delivering Citi’s most relevant guidance, offers, and content at every stage of their relationship. This innovative product is offered through a seamless end-to-end digital experience. Our talented teams within PBWM Technology redesigned key features like the Citi.com Homepage and Dashboard while building and leveraging scalable frameworks. This has helped expand multi-relationship customer population, increase overall digital engagement, expand data-driven personalized dynamic experiences, and more. The results of this Citi-built browser trading experience include enhanced engagement through a unified browsing and search experience for Trading, Insights, and News. Additionally, it provides clients with a deeper understanding of how they can build wealth with us.

First-to-Market Capabilities

Citi’s Custom Cash card empowers customers to earn and redeem rewards the way they want. Fully embracing Citi’s “Mobile First” vision, the Custom Cash technical team conceptualized, designed, and delivered first-to-market capabilities for customers to apply for a new credit card directly in an Apple App Clip, only a few months after App Clips launched on iOS 14. The teams leveraged HTML5 responsive design, containerized microservices, and mobile SDKs to deliver this and other intuitive capabilities for approved customers to begin using their card digitally, immediately upon approval, and to track rewards earned in real-time.

As the world’s most global bank, diversity is a top priority for us. We aspire to foster a welcoming, inclusive culture that attracts the best of the best. We’re continuing to focus on our aspirational goals to increase the representation of women and underrepresented groups by 2025. Our goal is to steadily progress the percentage of C12+ female talent represented in our global workforce by 43.5% and increase the representation of North American Black colleagues by 11.5%. We have a strong dedication to expand the U.S. representation of Hispanic and Latino colleagues by 16%. Similarly, we’re continuing to grow our talent by attracting Brazil Black and Pardo colleagues, with a goal to increase this group by 10%. Over in the U.K., we’re focusing on cultivating diversity, with goals to increase the representation of Asian colleagues by 11%, Black colleagues by 3% and mixed ethnicities by 3%.
